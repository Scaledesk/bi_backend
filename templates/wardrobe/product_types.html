What is Bloom Wardrobe?

Wardrobes are very essential part of bedroom/rooms.They increases the decor quotent as well as storage capacity in the room.Bloom Interio offers you extensive range of wardrobe design to keep your stuff organised and valuables safe.A well organised room look neat,spacious and gives peace of mind.Bloom wardrobes are designed to cater the needs of user according to their storage requirement.

We offer following type of wardrobe styles:-


Freestanding Wardrobes -

A cupboard shutter style wardrobe which comes in the range of dimensions fits in almost every space provided in the room.This style have medium storage capacity and it can be moved easily from one place to another.organisation and storage can be increased significantly by using suitable wardrobe accessories in them.


Built in wardrobes-

This type of wardrobes are meant to directly fit on the wall space.All the required measurements are taken to make a custom fit and style type is having more storage capacity.You can opt either for normal “cupboard-shutter type” or “sliding shutter”.Sliding shutters are more popular these days since they consume less space and more functional.You can also choose from a number of wardrobe accessories to increase storage space and better organisation of your clothes, shoes, coats and other nice items.Bloom Built in wardrobes are available in wide range of colours, designs and offer extensive flexibility to get along with any taste/style preferred by the customers.


Walk-in-Wardrobes-
