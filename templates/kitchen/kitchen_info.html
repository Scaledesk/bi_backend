MATERIALS

Plywood

A wooden sheet made of many thin layer of wood veneers comprised and glued together.

WPC

Wood Plastic Composite is made by mixing ground wood grains with heated thermoplastic resin.

HDF -High Density Fiberboard

A raw material product compression of wooden chip fibers generally with a glue at ah high temperature.HDF is stronger,harder and more water resistant than normal MDF.

Particle Board

Particle Board is also a type of engineered wood by compression of mixed wood chips,saw mill shavings or sawdust with resin.

Counter Tops

Kitchen Counter Tops can be made by variety of materials like Granite,Marble,Quartz,Corian, Soapstone,Steel, or Heritage wood.But most popular among them are Granite,Quartz nad corian whose description are as under:-

Granite

Granite is all-natural stone available in variety of colors,textures and price range.It is very strong,durable,heat resistant,cost effective,easy to maintain and anti bacterial.But it is not flexible ,hence can’t be moulded and porus material.

Quartz

Quartz are man-made granite look-alike stone made up of 90% quartz and remaining with polymers,resins and pigments.It is less porus than granite but a little hard to maintain.

Corian

This is totally engineered counter top material consist of acrylic polymer and aluminium trihydrate. The best feature of corian is that it’s very flexible,therefore ideal to use anywhere in the kitchen.But corian requires high maintenance,can get easily scratched and prone to get stained.
